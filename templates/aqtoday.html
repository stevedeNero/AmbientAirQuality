This Page Reports the Day's Air Quality Reading from Ambient Monitor's in Austin, Texas

Values of PM2.5 are in micrograms per cubic meter. Values of O3 (OZone) are in parts per billion.

All records are taken at a Continuous Ambient Monitoring Station (CAMS). The TCEQ operates CAMS sites at various metropolitan areas around the state.
